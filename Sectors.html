Retail:

Retail refers to the shops which are selling goods to the public for consumption.

Roles may include:

  • Sales associate
  • Cashier
  • Customer Assistant/Advisor
  • Visual Merchandiser
  • Buyer
  • Store Manager
  • Assistant Store Manager
  • Inventory Control Specialist
  • Shelf Fillers
  • Directors

Your role may have been automated due to robots stocking shelves, the increase in self checkouts or ecommerce becoming the new and convenient retail store.

Engineering and Manufacturing

Engineering and manufacturing is a branch of work which combines fields such as electrical, mechanical and industrial engineering. Manufacturing specifically refers to research, development, the processes as well as the equipment and facilities used for the production of quality goods.

Roles may include:

  • Building and Civil Engineering Technicians
  • Engineering Professionals
  • Aircraft Pilots and Flight Engineers
  • Production Managers
  • Production Directors
  • Planning, Process and Production Technicians
  • Warehouse Worker

Your role may have been automated due to machines being able to engineer, produce and fix merchandises. There are also computer systems in place which can fly a plane, without a pilot.

Transport and Logistics

Transport and Logistics is a two-step process. Transportation refers to the movement of products and materials from one place to another, such as shipments. Logistics is the management of this, ensuring that the correct quantity is shipped in a good condition and at the right price.

Roles may include:

  • Air Transport Operatives
  • Managers and Directors in Transport and Distribution
  • Other Drives and Operatives

Your role may have been automated due to online logistics, robot packers and fully automated warehouses, lead the way by Amazon and Ocado.

Accountancy, Business and Finance

Accountancy, Business and Finance is the combination of management, analysis and reporting of financial transactions in regard to business or personal.

Roles may include:

  • Financial Managers and Directors
  • Finance Officers
  • Business Sale Executives
  • Business and Financial Project Management Professionals
  • Chartered and Certified Accountants

Your role may have been automated due to digital records and because technology is able to collect, transform and visualise data.

Health Care

Health care consists of a wide range of services for the physically and mentally ill.

Roles may include:

  • Nurses
  • Health Professionals
  • Medical Practioners
  • Therapists
  • Dental
  • Pharmacists

Healthcare occupations have become automated because of repetitive clerical tasks are easily replicated by robots. Also, robotic surgery is now a thing of the future.

Law

The law sector consists of employees recognising and regulating the actions of public members according to the law. They also may imposition penalties on to those who disobey the law.

Roles may include:

  • Barrister
  • Barrister Clerk
  • Solicitor
  • Paralegal
  • Chartered Legal Assistant

Your role may have been automated due to AI now being able to conduct research, bill hours, inputting data as well as being able to conduct client interviews.

Public Services

Services which are provided to the public by the government. Providing safety, help and equal access to justice for the public and organisations.

Roles may include:

  • Fire Brigade
  • Police
  • Local Government
  • Civil Service
  • Teachers
  • Council Workers

Your public service role may have been automated due to AI being able to input data and automated speed cameras. Also, the rise of bots. Patrol, bomb disposable and knight scope security robots are now helping the public.

Leisure, Sport and Tourism

This sector covers a wide range of areas. Leisure and tourism refer to entertainment food as well as tourist goods and services. Sport can involve health and fitness training, activity work or by simply being a professional sports player.

Roles may include:

  • Waitresses
  • Trainers
  • Athletes
  • Cabin Crew
  • Tour Guides

Your job may have been automated because of automated food services and because of ecommerce.

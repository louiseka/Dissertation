Contact Us

At the Department for Automation and Remuneration we promise to solve any queries that you may have about our universal basic income scheme. We offer a free, confidential and independent service which is provided to you by our highly trained advisers. Supporting you from the early stages of your application to the end of your time with us.

If you are unable to use our digital services due to a disability, do not hesitate to contact us or have a next of kin get in touch with us on your behalf.

Report a Change

Your payments cease if you do not report a change to us immediately.

Changes to consider:

  • Getting a new job.
  • Moving in with your partner.
  • New address.
  • Becoming self employed.
  • Changing your bank details.
  • Change in life expectancy (becoming terminally ill and having less than 6 months to live).

If you are no longer out of work due to automation, you must let us know. Failure to report a change could result in you being taken to court or having to pay a penalty. So please let us know of any change in circumstances as soon as possible.

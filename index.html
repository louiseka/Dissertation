THE THREAT OF TECHNOLOGY ELIMINATING YOUR JOBS, VIA AUTOMATION HAS NOW BECOME A REALITY.

Find Out More

You may have been left without a guaranteed monthly income which has left you struggling to live your everyday life. At the Department for Automation and Remuneration (DAR), our job is to provide income to those who have lost their jobs to automation. We are here to help you to pay your monthly bills, to look after your family, and to kick start your reskilling journey.

Visit Our Services to learn more about us, and whether you are eligible for DAR's universal credit.
